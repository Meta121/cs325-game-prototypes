Revenge on Chickenzilla

  • Originality: In this 3rd person point of view 2D role-playing game (RPG), you have to navigate through an initial overworld to fight against the enemy. Once the player collides with the enemy in the overworld, the main game battle system begins where the player has health, special meter, and potions to manage while deciding which action to do. The game features a unique battle system where the player decides on what action he or she wants to do and then faces the consequences of those actions in retaliation by the enemy in a certain manner depending on what the player chooses. The game uniquely gives the player 4 buttons to do 4 options which effect how the player and enemy interact with each other. The player can only do the special move when his or her special meter is greater than or equal to 100. The player can also heal with potions but cannot when they have 0 potions. The game uniquely keeps track of a score of the player that increases or decreases depending on what action the player takes. The game has unique animations and sound effects depending on what action the player does. The game has a lot of original art but also some borrowed art. If a certain event happens, the game will change states.
  • Technical Merit: The game is able to create multiple objects that uniquely intereact with other objects within the game. The game has multiple scenes such as the start, overworld, main game, and end scenes which all have their individual purposes such as to introduce the game, to allow the player to explore an overworld, to let the player play battle system of the game, and to end the game. The game keeps track of variables that determine what occurs in the battle between the player and the enemy such the player resources like potions and what the current form of the chicken is. The game features an in-game restart feature by clicking R at the main game scene or any of the ending scenes to retry at the very start of the battle between the player and the enemy chicken. The game decides what occurs in the game depending on what the player decides to do. The game has unique animations and sound effects that change and occur depending on how the player interacts with the game.

The player must navigate through an overworld map to touch the Chickenzilla icon to start the battle between Claude and Chickenzilla. The player must deplete the health of Chickenzilla to 0 to defeat Chickenzilla to win the game. The player loses and gets a game over when the player character Claude health reaches 0. Claude can do some actions without the need of an outside resource. Claude can only do some specific actions if he has enough of a specific resource to use up.     • Overworld Gameplay - These are the gameplay elements of the overworld part of the game.
      • Press the up arrow key to move Claude up.
      • Press the up and left arrow key to move Claude up and left.
      • Press the up and right arrow key to move Claude up and right.
      • Press the left arrow key to move Claude left.
      • Press the right arrow key to move Claude right.
      • Press the down arrow key to move Claude down.
      • Press the down and left arrow key to move Claude down and left.
      • Press the down and right arrow key to move Claude down and right.
      • Make Claude touch Chickenzilla on the overworld to start the battle between him and Chickenzilla.
      • Press the R key to restart the game to the Claude vs. Chickenzilla of the game when in any scene besides the start scene.
    • Main Game Player vs. Chickenzilla Gameplay - These are the gameplay elements of the player vs. Chickenzilla part of the game.
      • Press the attack button to make Claude deal damage to Chickenzilla, take moderate damage from Chickenzilla, and fill up the special meter a medium amount.
      • Press the defend button to make Claude not deal damage to Chickenzilla, take less damage from Chickenzilla, and fill up the special meter a large amount.
      • Press the special button to make Claude deal a large amount of damage to Chickenzilla, take a larger than moderate amount of damage from Chickenzilla, and use up 100 points of the special meter if you have greater than or equal to 100 special meter points.
      • If you press the special button but do not have 100 special meter points then you just take damage and your turn is skipped.
      • Press the heal button to heal Claude for 500 points of health, to make Claude not deal damage to Chickenzilla, take a medium amount of damage from Chickenzilla, and fill up the special meter by a small amount.
      • If you press the heal button but do not have any potions left to use then you just take damage and your turn is skipped.
      • Press the R key to restart the game to the Claude vs. Chickenzilla of the game when in any scene besides the start scene.
    • Strategies: Take as much time as possible to defeat Chickenzilla because there is no time limit to complete the game in. Avoid using potions immediately and only use them when needed to obtain the most amount of score possible because using a potion lowers the amount of score the player has. Use the "Defend" button more than the "Attack" button to charge up the special meter to save more health while being able to use more special moves than if the player just kept on clicking the "Attack" button. Only use potions when the player is significantly not at max health because the health of the player cannot be increased pass the max health of the player.
